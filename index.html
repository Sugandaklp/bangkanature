Our Product

Banana Stem

Banana stem is a part banana tree. Using as a food, rich source of fibre and helps in weight loss.

I'ts high fibre content creates a feeling of satiation. It helps ease constipation.Banana stem is rich in potassium and vitamin B6 just like the fruit.

Banana Leaf

Banana leaves are leaves produced from the banana plant. Banana leaves have various uses for daily needs, such traditional cooking because they contain polyphenols so that they produce a distinctive aroma when used as complementary ingredients for culinary preparations. For handicrafts, banana leaves have flexible properties and are easy to shape into high-value works such as bags, wallets and others

Ambon Banana/Indonesian Cavendish

Cavendish banana is a tropical fruit commodity that is very popular in the world, in Indonesia, this banana is better known as Ambon banana . Ambon bananas are straight with a fairly thick skin.

The uniqueness of the Ambon banana is that it remains green even though it is ripe. But if it is too ripe the color is yellowish brown spots.

Kepok Banana

Kepok bananas are included in the processed banana group because of their high starch content.

Kepok bananas are usually processed into boiled, steamed or fried bananas and banana chips in Indonesia.
